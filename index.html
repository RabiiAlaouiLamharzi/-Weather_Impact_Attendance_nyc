Weather Conditions and Student Attendance
Patterns in New York City Schools (2018-2019)

WHAT

A visualization showing the relationship between weather conditions and student attendance patterns in NYC schools during 2018-2019.

WHO

This visualization is aimed at university students in New York City who are expected to attend classes throughout different months (September to June) and across varying weather conditions (temperatures and precipitation).

WHY

Students can explore the link between severe weather and attendance drops, helping them understand how environmental conditions affect school participation. This tool also allows students to compare their own attendance habits with citywide trends and recognize how weather challenges affect the broader student community.

HOW

Students can select their school from the dropdown menu to view its attendance data, hover over bars to see detailed information, and click on a month to filter the data by that month. The visualization displays attendance rates through stacked bars, temperature trends with a red line and precipitation levels with a blue line.
